About Braille World

Empowering accessibility through innovative technology

Our Mission

Braille World is dedicated to making digital content accessible to everyone, particularly those with visual impairments. We believe that accessibility should not be an afterthought but a fundamental right.

Through our innovative tools and technologies, we strive to bridge the gap between the digital world and the visually impaired community, ensuring that information and communication remain accessible to all.

What We Do

Text Conversion

Convert text to Braille with voice recognition and text-to-speech capabilities for seamless accessibility.

Image Processing

Extract text from images using advanced OCR technology with support for multiple languages including Hindi and English.

Accessibility Focus

Designed with accessibility in mind, ensuring compatibility across all devices and platforms including mobile applications.

Our Technology

  • Advanced OCR Technology
  • Speech Recognition
  • Text-to-Speech Synthesis
  • Unicode Braille Conversion
  • Multi-language Support
  • Cross-platform Compatibility

Our Values

Inclusivity
